Types Of Dogs

Dogs are divided into different types such as working dogs, hunting dogs, herding dogs, and companion dogs. Working dogs are used for guarding and rescue, while hunting dogs help in tracking. Herding dogs manage livestock, and companion dogs are kept as pets for love and friendship.
