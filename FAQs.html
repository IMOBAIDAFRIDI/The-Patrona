Questions about our website? Start here!

Why is vaccination important for puppies?
Vaccination protects against diseases such as Parvo, Distemper, and Rabies.

How often should I vaccinate?
Puppies: every 3–4 weeks until 16 weeks old. Adults: boosters every 1–3 years.

Can vaccination have side effects?
Mild fever, swelling, or tiredness may occur but usually fade in 24 hours.

How often should I bathe my dog?
Once every 4–6 weeks is best.

What’s the role of grooming?
Prevents tangles, reduces shedding, and detects skin issues.

What cleaning products are safe?
Use pet-safe shampoos. Avoid bleach/strong chemicals.

When to start training?
At 7–8 weeks with basics.

How to stop chewing and biting?
Redirect to chew toys, reward calm behavior.

How to train for loneliness?
Gradual absences, toy puzzles, calm departures.

How to create a pet-safe home?
Remove toxic plants, secure wires.

How to help with adjustment?
Offer patience, routine, and a safe corner.

How to handle separation anxiety?
Toys, calm exits, background music.

Best food for puppies?
High protein & vitamins.

Which foods are toxic?
Chocolate, grapes, onions, garlic.

How often to feed?
Puppies: 3–4 times/day. Adults: 2/day.

How to identify illness?
Watch for loss of appetite, vomiting, lethargy.

Do pets need annual exams?
Yes, vets check teeth, heart & vaccines.

What’s in a pet first-aid kit?
Bandages, antiseptic, thermometer, vet’s number.

What to know before adoption?
Consider budget, time & space.

How to prepare home?
Provide safe space, leash, toys.

How to build bond?
Play, walks & positive reinforcement.

How to keep my pet happy?
Balanced nutrition, daily walks.

How to travel with a dog safely?
Use crate, pack essentials, breaks.

What’s the best daily routine?
Fixed meals, 2 walks, rest.
